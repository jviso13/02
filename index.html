About Me

Hello! My name is Julian Viso. I was born in Santa Monica, California. After a brief stint living in Cuernavaca, Mexico, my family moved to Overland Park, Kansas. I attended the University of Kansas, graduating with a degree in political science. After graduation, I moved to Washington, D.C. and spent six years working for a foreign policy non-profit. I decided to move on from the political arena and am looking forward to a new career in web development. In my free time, I enjoy basketball, cooking, and collecting sneakers.
